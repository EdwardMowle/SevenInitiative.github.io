Storydock

My passion project for writers

I am the founder and lead developer of Storydock. A few years ago I committed myself to realising a childhood dream of becoming a published fiction writer. As I learnt about how to write and structure fiction from books, courses and seminars I realised so much of this wisdom could be embedded in an application that would give fiction writers the kinds of features that I had taken for granted, as a coder, when using my favourite IDE (code editor). I couldn't find it, so I built it.

Storydock helps it's users to organise their notes, create their cast of characters, structure their plot (no matter how complex), write their novel with automatic hints to aid readability or link to their notes, and ultimately publish it to an online audience.

Please visit storydock.io to learn more.
